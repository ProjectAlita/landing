Our professional services around AI

ELITEA is a platform designed to streamline the management, development, and collaboration of Large Language Model (LLM) assets within organizations by embedding ELITEA into common interfaces and tools.

SERVICE

EngX AI Transformation Program

A holistic AI transformation program targeted on the increase of time-to-value and the reduction of cost-to-value. Starts with EngX Assessment to outline a GenAI transformation plan and drive implementation.

SERVICE

AI empowered testing as a service

Our Testing as a Service offering includes AI components that expedite the generation of test scripts, issue triage, execution results analysis, and the development of test automation scripts, among others.

SERVICE

PoC of Gen-AI on use-cases

Aimed at implementing PoCs and try-out cases. This includes implementation of individual use cases or a set of use cases within the specified number of teams. It also allows for training and trials to assess the impact of AI on productivity.

SERVICE

AI agent development

Implementation of custom GenAI Agents and Assistants to augment and uplift user experience in cases, such as: support request handling and triage, Q&A, using specific terms and data, etc.
